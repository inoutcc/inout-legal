Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

in/out ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the in/out mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Health and fitness data: calorie intake, food logs, weight entries, exercise logs, sleep data, step count, heart rate, and calorie burn — entered manually or synced from Android Health Connect.
  • App activity: XP earned, streaks, missions completed, goals set, and feature usage.
  • Device information: device type and operating system version, used only for app compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the app experience.
  • To sync your data across devices via our secure cloud backend (Supabase).
  • To power AI features including food analysis, meal planning, and daily insights (processed via Groq AI).
  • To calculate calorie goals, streaks, XP, and progress tracking.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Health Connect Data

in/out integrates with Android Health Connect to read the following data types:

  • Steps
  • Active and total calories burned
  • Distance
  • Heart rate and resting heart rate
  • Heart rate variability
  • Sleep sessions
  • Body weight

Health Connect data is used solely to display your fitness activity within the app and to calculate your daily calorie balance. This data is never shared with third parties and is only transmitted to our secure backend for the purpose of syncing your personal dashboard.

in/out does not use Health Connect data for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. AI Processing

When you use AI-powered features (food photo analysis, voice logging, restaurant lookup, meal planning, daily insights), text or image data describing your food or health context is sent to Groq AI for processing. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests. Groq's privacy policy is available at groq.com/privacy-policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account and health data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Row-level security ensures only you can access your own data. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the app's Settings screen. Upon deletion, all your personal data and health records are permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("Delete My Account" in Settings).
  • Withdraw consent for Health Connect data at any time via Android Settings → Health Connect → App permissions.

8. Children's Privacy

in/out is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
